About Us
Nairobi's Trusted Package Hub

Nyakiba DropPoint was created to solve a real problem in Nairobi — packages that get missed, lost, or left at the wrong address. We are a dedicated package pickup point where businesses can drop off deliveries and customers can collect them at their convenience.
